Project Name: RoboShow ‚Äì Robotics Project Showcase & Feedback Platform

1. Product Overview

RoboShow is a web-based platform that enables students and teams to showcase their robotics projects and receive structured feedback through ratings and comments.

The system simulates a real-world technical exhibition and portfolio platform used in academic and professional environments. It focuses on visibility, peer evaluation, and performance analytics.

The interface is clean, card-based, and optimized for desktop usage.

2. Goals & Learning Objectives
Primary Goals
